About Me

Frontend Developer

Hello! I'm a second-year B.Tech student at GLA University, currently pursuing my degree with a keen interest in front-end development. My journey in the world of technology began in my first semester, where I was introduced to the fundamentals of C programming. This initial exposure ignited my passion for coding and problem-solving.

In my second semester, I expanded my skill set by learning Python, which further solidified my love for programming. During a two-month break, I took the opportunity to dive into web development and acquired proficiency in HTML, CSS, and Java. Additionally, I ventured into Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) and successfully solved 50 questions on LeetCode, enhancing my problem-solving skills.

My dedication and hard work paid off when I secured an internship with CodSoft, where I am currently gaining hands-on experience in front-end development. This internship has provided me with valuable insights and practical knowledge that I am eager to apply to real-world projects.

In addition to my technical skills, I am passionate about continuous learning and staying updated with the latest industry trends. My goal is to become a proficient front-end developer and contribute to innovative projects that make a difference.
